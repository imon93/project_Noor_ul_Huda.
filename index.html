Our Story

Rooted in faith, built by community

Since 1991, Masjid Noor-ul-Huda has grown from a single rented room to a full community mosque, entirely through the generosity of local families. Every brick, every carpet, every lesson taught here carries a piece of this neighborhood's story.

Today we host five daily prayers, weekend Islamic school, a food pantry, and a space where new Muslims and long-time residents alike can learn and grow together.
